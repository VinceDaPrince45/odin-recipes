French Toast

Description

A sweet delectable breakfast that is often affiliated with a common food item, bread. French toast is tender, fluffy, and indulgent, made with only five ingredients that most people will have in their kitchen

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla and cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a bowl
  2. Lighly butter griddle or skillet over medium high heat
  3. Dunk bread in egg mixture, making sure it soaks
  4. Place the bread onto the hot skillet and cook on both sides until golden brown
